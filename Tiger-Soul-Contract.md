---

## Overview

Lauren Burandt will design, build, and maintain the Tiger Soul website and member
platform. The work is delivered in the phases below. Each phase builds on the last,
so the site can go live and start taking payments early while the rest is built out.

---

## Scope of Work

### Phase 1 — Website & Programs
- Full website build with a dedicated page for each **program**
- Online **payments** on every program (deposits or paid in full)
- Mobile-friendly, on-brand design

### Phase 2 — Membership Portal
A private, logged-in area where each member can:
- Make and manage **payments**
- See the **programs they're enrolled in**
- View their **credits** (e.g. coaching sessions) and included **resources**

### Phase 3 — Admin Backend (Staff Dashboard)
One private dashboard for Tiger Soul staff to:
- Track **upcoming appointments** and who they belong to
- See **who has and hasn't paid** for each package
- Manage **scheduling** — coaching sessions, calendars, appointments
- Open a member's account to issue **refunds, credits, or granted sessions**

### Phase 4 — Automated Reminders
- Automated **email and text (SMS)** reminders for appointments and payments
- Sent to **members/clients** and to **staff/admin**

### Phase 5 — Merchandise Shop
- Full online store for Tiger Soul merchandise, separate from programs

---

## How It's Built

The platform is assembled from proven, secure services — **Stripe** for payments,
refunds, and credits; a scheduling system for the calendar and appointments; and an
email/SMS service for reminders — with a **custom-designed website, member portal,
and staff dashboard** built on top. This keeps the build faster, more reliable, and
lower-cost than building everything from scratch.

---

**$3,000 per month.**

- Third-party service costs (payment processing fees, website hosting, text/email
  sending, domain) are **paid directly by Tiger Soul** and are separate from this rate.

- Phase order and priorities can be adjusted by mutual agreement.
