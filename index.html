About

A guide who has walked the path before you.

Blaine founded Tiger Soul after his own journey through the darkness and back into the light. What began as personal survival became a sacred vocation: to walk beside others as they reclaim their courage and remember their wholeness.

Every retreat, ceremony, and training is shaped by lived experience, deep respect for the medicines, and an unwavering commitment to your safety and sovereignty.
