I have to admit that I was little reluctant and afraid to do Bufo. Even though my previous sessions with different types of plant medicine was nothing but loving, inspiring and above all positive, I was still holding on to try Bufo.

Everything changed when I met Blaine and felt his energy and good intentions. When I finally felt that it's the right moment I put aside all fears and came with open mind and heart for the Bufo ceremony.

Blaine, with all his experience and care, guided me through the most amazing journey of my life. He also helped me afterwards to process and understand everything that was presented to me. I'm forever grateful to meet Blaine and the powerful medicine he shared with me. My life is completely changed for good and I will never forget that day in Tulum.

Since then, Blaine worked with many of my friends and all of them had extremely positive feedback. Thank you Blaine for everything.
