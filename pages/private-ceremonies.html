Private Ceremonies

Sacred healing, one soul at a time

Intimate, one-on-one medicine ceremonies — held with prayer, intention, and deep, heart-centered guidance.

Bufo · Kambo · Rapéh · Fully Private

Discover Your Inner Peace

Some journeys are meant to be walked alone, in a space held entirely for you. A private ceremony is a sacred one-on-one container — tailored to your story, your readiness, and your intention — where the medicine and the facilitator meet you exactly where you are.

Each medicine holds a unique spirit, purpose, and wisdom. Together we choose the ally that calls to you, and hold it with reverence, safety, and love.

Medicine of the Gods

Sacred Medicine

Bufo

A powerful sacred medicine known for its profound healing benefits — deep emotional release, spiritual awakening, and a felt connection to the divine.

In a private Bufo ceremony, you step into a sacred space of profound healing and awakening, where the ancient wisdom of the medicine meets deep, heart-centered guidance.

Sacred Medicine

Kambo

An ancient Amazonian medicine used to cleanse the body, mind, and spirit — promoting deep detoxification and spiritual renewal.

In a private Kambo ceremony, the powerful medicine of the Amazon guides you through deep physical, emotional, and spiritual cleansing.

Sacred Medicine

Rapéh

A sacred Amazonian snuff — known for its grounding, clearing, and centering effects, woven into ceremonial practice as a tool for deep presence and prayer.

Received one-on-one and guided with prayer and intention, a private Rapéh ceremony is a sacred space for grounding and deepening your connection to self.
