About Blaine

A guide who has walked the path

Founder & facilitator — trained in the Shipibo, Kambo, and Bufo lineages, walking beside others on the journey from darkness into light.

The Founder

Blaine discovered his gift for energetic release in ceremony — through a deep communion with Ayahuasca and time spent with Shipibo Maestros in Peru.

In the medicine, he found he could sense and remove negative energies, entities, traumas, and blockages held in the body's energy centers — often feeling another person's purge before it arrived, and purging on their behalf. The shamans who witnessed this recognized what he carried, saying he held "the spirit of the white dove, helping to cleanse the energy within the circle."

His Way of Working

Reverence, non-duality, and return

His Bufo training deepened an understanding of non-duality and ego dissolution — guiding others toward peace and unity. He works with deep reverence for the medicine, helping each person release what no longer serves them and reconnect with their true essence.

He has held space for the healing of ancestral trauma, addiction, depression, PTSD, and physical suffering — witnessing people move from suicidal ideation to a genuine appreciation for life.
