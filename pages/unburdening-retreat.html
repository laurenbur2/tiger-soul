The Unburdening Retreat

Set down what you've carried

Three days in Tulum to release the weight of the year before the holidays begin — and to meet the season, and the New Year, as someone who chose what to carry forward.

Bufo · 5-MeO-DMT · Tulum, México · December 4–6, 2026

Apply Now

Before the Year Turns

The end of the year has a way of catching up with you. The calendar fills, the table gets set, and everyone you've ever been is expected to show up and be glad. For many of us, December is when the heaviest things surface — a loss, a grief, a version of ourselves we never quite put down.

The Unburdening is a chance to set that weight down before the holidays, rather than carry it through them. Three days, a small group, and one of the most direct medicines on earth — held with care, so you walk into the season lighter and into the New Year clear.

Why December

The season asks a lot of you

Most of the year you can outrun what you're holding. The holidays are when it catches up — old wounds, family, the ache of another year gone. This retreat makes room to meet it on your own terms first, so you arrive at the table as yourself, not as everything you've been carrying.

  • Release grief and old wounds before the holidays
  • Loosen what the season tends to stir up
  • Close the year with intention, not exhaustion
  • Step into the New Year with a clearer mind

The Three Days

Arrive heavy, leave lighter

A gentle arc — settle, then release, then integrate — so the work has somewhere to land before you head home.

FridayArrival

You land, and the noise stops

The evening is for slowing down — an opening circle, gentle breath and movement, and space to name what you came to set down. You'll meet your facilitators and the small group walking beside you. Nothing is asked of you today but arriving.

SaturdayThe Medicine

Sitting with Bufo

The heart of the retreat. You'll sit with Bufo (5-MeO-DMT), one to one and fully held. The experience is short in minutes and vast in scope, and what meets you there tends to be exactly what's been underneath the weight you've carried. The rest of the day stays quiet — rest, nourishment, integration, the ocean close by.

SundayIntegration

The day most retreats skip

We don't. Through circle and one-on-one time, you'll find language for what happened and something you can actually use — because in a few weeks you're walking back into the same rooms, and this time you get to choose what you bring with you.

The Container

Held from the moment you arrive

A small, carefully tended group and a team that stays close — so you can give your full attention to the work and none to the logistics.

  • A small, intimately held group
  • One-to-one Bufo ceremony with trained facilitators
  • Opening circle and personal intention-setting
  • Integration circle and a one-on-one session
  • Nourishing meals and unhurried rest
  • Guidance to carry the work home with you

Begin

End the year lighter

Spaces are limited and held with care. Reach out to claim yours — every journey begins with a simple health screening, so your safety and readiness are honored from the start.
