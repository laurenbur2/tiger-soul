Health Screening

Every application begins here, with a simple health screening — so we can meet you safely, and hold you well.

Step One · Your Application

Why We Ask

Thank you for your interest in applying to Tiger Soul Retreats. Before we speak, we ask everyone to complete a short health screening. It tells us how to care for you, and whether this path is right for you at this moment in your life.

There are no wrong answers here, and nothing you share will be held against your application. We ask because we take your safety seriously.

Our Mission

Reduce unnecessary suffering by helping people heal emotionally, mentally, physically, and spiritually — through education, community, ceremony, and ongoing integration.

What Happens Next

How your application unfolds

No automated replies, and no rush. Every application is read personally.

FirstThe Screening

You share where you are

A short set of questions about your health, your history, and any medications you take. It stays between you and the facilitators.

ThenWe Read It

We review it personally

Blaine reads every screening himself. If anything needs a closer look, we'll ask — gently, and without judgment.

NextWe Speak

We talk, soul to soul

A personal conversation to answer your questions and feel into whether this is your time. You are never obligated to continue.

FinallyYour Place

Your place is held

If the path is aligned, we'll walk you through preparation, dates, and everything you need to arrive ready.
