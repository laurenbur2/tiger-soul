Bufo Practitioner Training

Step into the path of Sacred Service

An 11-day immersive initiation into the Bufo lineage — carrying this medicine with integrity, skill, and deep reverence.

Luntia Jungle Retreat · Puerto Morelos, Mexico · March 6–18, 2027

A Sacred Initiation

This is not just a certification — it is a sacred initiation into the Bufo lineage. An intimate, immersive retreat that blends ancestral wisdom, hands-on facilitation practice, and profound personal transformation.

With quality over quantity and deeply individualized attention, you will be prepared to hold space for others with the skill and reverence this medicine demands.

The Journey

Two movements, one initiation

First you heal. Then you learn to hold the medicine for others.

Days 1–5Personal Healing

Clearing & Becoming a Pure Vessel

Deep personal healing and spiritual hygiene to create a clear, pure vessel for service — connecting with the Bufo spirit, aligning with its wisdom, and learning sacred container creation, prayer, intention, and energetic protection.

Days 6–11Facilitation & Mastery

Holding Space with Integrity

Client intake and trust-building, ceremony observation and hands-on experience, administering Bufo with safety and integrity, preparing ceremonial space and energetic balance, and guiding integration for those you serve.
