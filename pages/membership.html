Questions

Before you join

Why do I need to become a member?

To participate in any retreat, ceremony, or support group, you must first become a member. Membership is the sacred and legal container that allows us to hold this work with integrity and care.

What does the $25 membership include?

One annual membership grants full access — our sacred medicines, ongoing support and resources, and eligibility to attend every retreat, ceremony, and gathering we offer.

Does membership cover the cost of retreats?

Membership makes you eligible to attend and unlocks access to our offerings. Individual retreats, ceremonies, and trainings are booked separately — membership is your first step onto the path.

How long does membership last?

Membership is annual — $25 for a full year within the Tiger Soul community, renewable each year as you continue your journey with us.
