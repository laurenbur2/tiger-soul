Sample Journey

Six days of transformation

A rhythm of ceremony, integration, and rest — shaped around you. Dates are flexible and arranged privately.

Day OneArrival

Arrival in Tulum

Private shuttle from the airport, a welcome dinner, and settling into your sanctuary.

Day TwoOpening

Opening Ceremony & Kambo

Prayer and offerings to the land, a microdose of Bufo to connect with Spirit, and a Kambo ceremony for deep cleansing. Evening sound bath.

Day ThreeIntegration

Integration & Exploration

Morning meditation, breathwork, and yoga. Integration with Mambe, then free time to explore Tulum or rest.

Day FourJourney

Deep Journey — Bufo & Mushrooms

Voice toning and sound healing, afternoon breathwork, and an evening mushroom ceremony with integration.

Day FiveRestoration

Restoration & Reflection

A slow morning, an integration session and your chosen healing modality, and time at the ocean, cenotes, or sacred sites.

Day SixClosing

Closing Ceremony & Farewell

A closing ceremony and gratitude offerings, breakfast at a local favorite, and a shuttle to the airport.
